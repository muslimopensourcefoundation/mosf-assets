Volunteer Role Title:
Communications & Operations Coordinator (Volunteer)
Reports to:
Founder & Executive Director, Muslim Open Source Foundation
Commitment:
5–10 hours per week (flexible, remote, volunteer basis)
Overview
The Muslim Open Source Foundation (MOSF) is a newly forming nonprofit based in Washington State, USA, dedicated to empowering the global Muslim community through open-source collaboration, education, and ethical technology.
As the Communications & Operations Coordinator, you’ll support the early growth of MOSF by helping shape its voice, maintain its professional presence, and streamline day-to-day communication and coordination. This is a volunteer position offering the chance to contribute meaningfully from the foundation’s earliest stages and grow alongside its mission.
Key Responsibilities
* Communications & Branding
   * Develop and maintain consistent messaging across MOSF platforms (website, LinkedIn, email).
   * Draft and format updates, press releases, and social media posts aligned with MOSF’s mission and tone.
   * Assist in creating simple digital assets using Canva or similar tools.
* Operational Support
   * Manage shared inboxes, calendars, and document organization for the foundation.
   * Prepare meeting summaries, track action items, and maintain volunteer documentation.
   * Support onboarding and coordination of future volunteers and collaborators.
* Public Relations & Outreach
   * Support outreach efforts with potential partners, educators, and open-source contributors.
   * Draft correspondence and maintain records of outreach initiatives.
   * Coordinate logistics for online sessions or announcements.
Ideal Candidate
* Demonstrates strong written communication and attention to detail.
* Familiar with social media management and Canva design tools.
* Organized, reliable, and proactive in asynchronous remote environments.
* Passionate about Islamic values, education, and open-source collaboration.
* Comfortable volunteering time for a mission-driven initiative at its formation stage.
Benefits
* Direct mentorship from the founder and exposure to nonprofit startup operations.
* Opportunity to shape the foundation’s communications identity from the ground up.
* Letter of recommendation and recognition on the MOSF website as an inaugural volunteer contributor.
* Potential transition to a paid or leadership role post-funding, insha Allah.
