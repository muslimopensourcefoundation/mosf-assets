Volunteer Role Title:
Mobile App Developer (Volunteer)
Reports to:
Founder & Executive Director, Muslim Open Source Foundation
Commitment:
5–10 hours per week (flexible, remote, volunteer basis)
Overview
The Muslim Open Source Foundation (MOSF) is a nonprofit based in Washington State, USA, dedicated to empowering the global Muslim community through open-source collaboration, education, and ethical technology.
As a Mobile App Developer (Volunteer), you’ll play a key role in transforming MOSF’s educational vision into a cross-platform mobile experience. You'll help design and develop the mobile application for MOSF's first project, the Basic Arabic Reading App, a self-paced program designed to help English speakers learn to read Arabic from scratch.
This is a volunteer position offering the opportunity to contribute meaningfully from MOSF's earliest stages and grow alongside its mission.
Key Responsibilities
Mobile Development & Integration
* Develop and maintain cross-platform mobile applications using Flutter and Dart.
* Implement responsive and accessible UI designs with a focus on seamless user experience across Android and iOS.
* Integrate frontend components with backend APIs and authentication services.
* Ensure mobile apps adhere to secure coding, performance optimization, and data privacy best practices.
* Contribute to establishing mobile development standards, documentation practices, and reusable components for future MOSF projects.
Collaboration & Open-Source Contribution
* Work closely with frontend and backend volunteers to ensure consistent performance and design across web and mobile platforms.
* Participate in code reviews, issue tracking, and project discussions within MOSF’s open-source repositories.
* Contribute to onboarding materials and assist in mentoring future mobile contributors as the team expands.
Accessibility & Multilingual Support
* Collaborate with the frontend team to implement Arabic (RTL) and multilingual capabilities within mobile interfaces.
* Ensure usability and readability for learners of diverse technical and linguistic backgrounds.
* Explore mobile frameworks or libraries that enhance accessibility, interactivity, and learning engagement.
Ideal Candidate
* Proficient in Flutter and Dart, with experience deploying apps to Android and iOS.
* Familiar with Firebase, RESTful APIs, and cross-platform integrations.
* Strong understanding of secure mobile development and state management.
* Comfortable working with Git-based collaboration and open-source workflows.
* Passionate about Islamic education, open knowledge, and ethical technology.
* Organized, reliable, and proactive in asynchronous, remote environments.
* Excited to help shape the mobile foundation of a mission-driven nonprofit from the ground up.
Benefits
* Direct collaboration with the Founder & Executive Director.
* Opportunity to lead the development of MOSF’s first mobile app for global learners.
* Recognition on the MOSF website as an inaugural volunteer contributor.
* Letter of recommendation and potential for future leadership or paid roles post-funding, insha Allah.
